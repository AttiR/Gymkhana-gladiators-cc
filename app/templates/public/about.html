History

Helsinki Gymkhana Cricket Club ry is officially registered with Finnish Cricket Association (FCA) known in Finnish as Suomen krikettiliiton and Finnish Patentti ja Rekisterihallitus (PRH). Registration number is 209.205. Helsinki Gymkhana Cricket Club ry was founded on 21.12.2012. Hgymkhana cc Helsinki based cricket club. The main objective of Hgymkhanaclub ry is to play and promote good quality and friendly cricket in Finalnd. To achieve this objective management is motivated to coordinate with Finland Cricket Association, VALO and other cricket clubs in Finland. We welcome people from all nationalities, back grounds and cultures without any discramination.

Why to Join Gymkhana?

WELCOME!   Cricket is in our blood. Feel the same? Come and see us in action. Or try out and play with us!   Mission Statement: ​ To provide the opportunity for all people, regardless of Age, Race, Religion, Gender, Culture, Disabilities, or Background to play quality cricket in a friendly environment in the true spirit of the game. ​ HGCC became champions of Finnish Premier League 2020 and One Day Cup 2020 both, its really great achievement and honour for all members.
